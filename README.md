# Emoji Speedway

A small browser racing game where emoji drivers race emoji cars around an oval track against AI opponents.

## How to play

1. Open `index.html` in a browser.
2. Pick your racer from the roster.
3. Press **Start Race**.
4. Hold **Arrow Up** or **W** to accelerate.
5. Hold **Arrow Down** or **S** to brake.
6. Tap **Arrow Left / Right** or **A / D** to change lanes.
7. Finish 3 laps before the AI drivers to win.
